What are emulators?

Emulators serve as software tools that enable one system to replicate the functionality of another. Numerous individuals have developed freeware emulators, enabling your personal computer to mimic various video game consoles. Although emulators are not flawless and cannot fully replicate the authentic experience, they often come remarkably close. Within The Vault, you can access downloads for popular emulators tailored to different gaming systems.
